How Power Score is Calculated

The Power Score (0–1200) measures overall weekly dominance across all 12 scoring categories.

  • For each category, every team is ranked from best to worst.
  • The best team scores 100, worst scores 0, others scale linearly.
  • Scores for all 12 categories are summed → max possible is 1,200.
  • ERA, WHIP, and TB are lower is better — the team with the lowest value scores 100.

The chart uses Running Power Score: cumulative season-to-date stats are normalized each week, so it reflects who has been strongest overall rather than just in the latest week.

Power Rank is simply the week-by-week ranking (1 = best that week).
